Deploying large language models for enterprise tasks like Request-for-Proposal (RFP) completion is hindered by hallucinations and lack of response provenance. This paper presents an improved RAG architecture that significantly reduces manual effort in drafting responses while maintaining verifiability. We introduce a novel hybrid embedding scheme that combines dense contextual embeddings with sparse statistical embeddings, reducing the no-context rate by 83.33%. Additionally, we present an algorithm that accurately attributes document page numbers as references, enabling human reviewers to quickly verify and correct generated responses. This work demonstrates a practical, deployable solution for automated document completion where efficiency, accuracy, and verifiability are paramount.
