Presented my research on improving Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG) systems for enterprise document completion at the 4th AIMLSystems Conference. The talk addressed key challenges in deploying LLMs for Request-for-Proposal (RFP) workflows, where accuracy and verifiability are critical for legally binding documents.

I introduced two novel contributions: a hybrid embedding scheme that combines dense contextual embeddings with sparse statistical embeddings to capture both semantic meaning and domain-specific terminology, and a Document Page Finder algorithm that provides precise source attribution for generated responses. Together, these improvements reduced the no-context rate by 83%, significantly enhancing both retrieval accuracy and human reviewability.
